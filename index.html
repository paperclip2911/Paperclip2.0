❓ HOW TO PLAY

🎮 Controls

WASD / Arrow keys to move
Click or tap to attack
F key = special ability
Space = dodge roll
E = talk to NPCs
I = inventory, M = map
1-5 = use hotbar items

⚔ Combat

Approach enemies to fight
Dodge to avoid damage
Use potions to heal
Kill enemies for XP + Gold
Level up to get stronger
Watch your stamina bar

🗺 World

Start at Heartwood Village
Explore 8 territories
Each zone has unique enemies
Bosses guard each region
Hell & Abyss = endgame zones
Day/night cycle active

🌟 Magic Man

A mysterious figure hides
somewhere far from the village.
Very very hard to find.
He grants permanent power
for 50 gold. Most adventurers
never find him...

⚜ Clans

Join one of 4 clans
Each gives unique bonuses
Iron = ATK + DEF
Arcane = Magic power
Shadow = Speed + Crit
Flame = Fire damage

🧪 Potions

HP Potion = restore health
MP Potion = restore mana
Strength = attack boost
Speed = movement boost
Magic Surge = spell power
Invincibility = rare & powerful

⚔ CHOOSE YOUR RACE
Your race defines your destiny
⚜ JOIN A CLAN
Clans fight for territory. Choose wisely.
